The short version: almost all of your data stays on your device. We do not operate servers, do not collect analytics, and do not have access to your health information.

1. Overview

Daily Structure Tracker is a daily task and symptom tracking application designed for individuals with AuDHD (Autism + ADHD), bipolar disorder, and other neurodivergent conditions. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, where it is stored, and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect and Where It Is Stored

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All of the following is stored exclusively in your device's local browser storage (localStorage and IndexedDB). It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

  • Daily task completion records
  • Mood and energy level logs
  • Symptom flags
  • Personal notes entered during check-ins
  • Medication streak counter
  • App configuration (your name, task lists, symptom flags, notification preferences)
  • Accessibility preferences (theme, text size, contrast, motion settings)
  • Appointment records and notes
  • Custom timer settings
  • Saved favourite quotes
  • Audio files upoaded for custom notification sounds
  • Screenshot save folder preferences

This data is retained on your device until you clear your browser data or uninstall the app. We have no ability to access, recover, or delete this data on your behalf.

2.2 Data Transmitted to Third-Party Services (Optional Features)

The following features are entirely optional and involve data leaving your device. None are enabled by default and all require explicit setup by you.

Telegram Notifications

If you choose to connect a Telegram bot, the app will send notification messages to your bot using the Telegram Bot API. These messages may include your name and the names of incomplete tasks. Your Telegram bot token and chat ID are stored locally on your device. We do not store or have access to these credentials.

AI-Generated Messages (Anthropic API)

If you choose to enable AI-generated encouragement messages, the app will send limited contextual data to the Anthropic API to generate personalised messages. This data includes:

  • Your display name (if set)
  • Today's task completion percentage
  • Today's mood and energy levels
  • Number and names of flagged symptoms
  • Current medication streak

This data is sent to Anthropic's servers to generate a response and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Your Anthropic API key is stored locally on your device. We do not store or have access to your API key. This feature requires you to create your own Anthropic account and obtain your own API key.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contact information (other than what you voluntarily enter)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Google Play / Apple App Store)
  • Any data through advertising networks

The app contains no advertising, no tracking pixels, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no telemetry of any kind.

4. Health and Sensitive Data

This application is designed to help users track symptoms related to mental health conditions including bipolar disorder, ADHD, autism spectrum conditions, and related diagnoses. The symptom, mood, and medication data you log is considered sensitive health information.

As described above, this data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party unless you explicitly use the optional features described in Section 2.2.

This app is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. It is a personal tracking tool. Please consult your healthcare providers for medical advice.

5. Screenshot and Image Save Features

The app includes a feature to capture and save a record of your symptom check-in as an image. This image is generated entirely on your device using your locally stored data. The image is saved to your device's storage using your device's native file system or share functionality.

We do not receive, store, or have access to any images created by this feature.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used this app, please contact us at jake.work456@gmail.com.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected about them and to request deletion of that information.

Because all personal data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or have access to it, we are unable to provide, correct, or delete data on your behalf. You can delete all app data at any time by clearing your browser storage or uninstalling the app.

We do not sell personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on the app listing page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
